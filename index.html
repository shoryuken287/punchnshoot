Controls

Player 1:
  • Move Up: W
  • Move Left: A
  • Move Down: S
  • Move Right: D
  • Punch: C
  • Shoot: V

Player 2:
  • Move Up: ↑
  • Move Left: ←
  • Move Down: →
  • Move Right: ↓
  • Punch: O
  • Shoot: P

About the Game

Punch & Shoot is a free game with simple controls. It will keep you entertained for hours and even includes play with friends. There are multiple modes to play with your friends as well as multiple characters to choose from!
